Cloud platform user guide

Who is this user guide for?

This user guide is for the teams who deployed their applications in Ministry of Justice Cloud platform or want/migrate to deploy and manage their applications on the cloud platform. The platform manages the infrastructure that your application runs on and provides tooling for teams to deploy and manage their applications on that infrastructure.
